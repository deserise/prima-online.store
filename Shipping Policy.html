Shipping policy

- All orders ship from our warehouse in Delta, British Columbia, Canada.

- Once your order has been placed, you will receive an order confirmation email confirming your order. Please allow up to 2-3 business days for your order to be packed and picked up by the courier.

- When your order is shipped, you will receive a shipping confirmation to the email provided at time of checkout which will include tracking information for your parcel.

- prefertake.com is not responsible for delays incurred during transit.

- When your parcel is delivered, a delivery confirmation will be issued. prefertake.com is not responsible for the parcel once the item's tracking status states delivered in any manner of the term. This includes, but is not limited to the terms "safe dropped", "delivered to door", and "delivery notice card left". prefertake.com is not responsible if a package is lost or stolen.

- International shipments are subject to duty, customs and tax charges. Additional charges are distinguished when your parcel crosses the border and are the responsibility of the customer. If the value of your order is under $800 CAD, it will exempt of duty charges.

- If your package is returned to us because the wrong shipping address was provided, it was refused or import/duty fees were left un-paid, you will be refunded for the item minus the original shipping fee charged by the courier on the order and any and all return shipping fees. 

-If you were shipped the wrong item, please contact us here. Bare Knitwear will arrange for a replacement item to be shipped and the customer will incur no charges.

- If your item arrives damaged, please contact us here with photos of the damaged item and the package which your item arrived in immediately. If prefertake.com agrees the item was damaged in transit, a replacement item will be shipped to the customer at no additional cost.  

Shipping Domestic:

- Complimentary shipping on all domestic orders over $200 CAD.
- Standard and Express shipping options provided.
- prefertake.com ships to all provinces via Canada Post and FedEx.
- Estimated Standard transit time varies based on destination. 

Standard Transit Times:

- British Columbia to Saskatchewan: 1-5 Business Days
- Ontario to Quebec: 4-9 Business Days
- Maritimes: 5-13 Business Days

Express Shipping:

- Express shipments will ship exclusively through FedEx.
